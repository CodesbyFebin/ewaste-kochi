The Processing Gap in Municipal E-Waste

The Haritha Karma Sena teams do a phenomenal job collecting plastics, dry waste, and residential e-waste from across Kochi. However, electronic waste contains harmful neurotoxins—lead, mercury, cadmium—that cannot be processed in standard Material Recovery Facilities (MRFs).

The Solution: EWasteKochi acts as the specialized endpoint. When local panchayats, resident associations, or Haritha workers consolidate old CRT monitors, broken appliances, and obsolete desktop computers, we dispatch massive logistics fleets to transport this directly to our certified dismantling plant in Thrippunithura.

This prevents illegal scrapping, acid burning by informal dealers, and prevents heavy metals from leaking into Kerala's backwaters.
