Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to questions about our ITAD, data destruction, and e-waste process.

What is ITAD?
IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) is the process of safely managing the disposal, recycling, or reuse of obsolete IT equipment in a safe and ecologically-responsible manner.
Do you give a Certificate of Destruction?
Yes, we provide a comprehensive, serialized Certificate of Destruction (CoD) for every single wiped, degaussed, or shredded data-bearing device.
Are you DPDP Act compliant?
Yes. India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 requires explicit verifiable destruction of data. Our NIST 800-88 process and certified paperwork ensure full compliance.
Are you PCB authorized?
Yes, we are fully authorized by the Kerala State Pollution Control Board under the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2022.
