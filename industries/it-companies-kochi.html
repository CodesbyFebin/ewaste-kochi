The "Format" Fallacy
Simply deleting or formatting offboarded employee laptops does not erase data blocks. Software recovery tools can pull source code, PII, and API keys in minutes.

Handing old servers to local scrap dealers means losing chain of custody. If their drives end up on the secondary market, the originating company is fully liable.
