Why Legal Disposal Matters

Improper E-Waste Disposal Is Illegal in Kerala

  • E-Waste Management Rules 2022 require all businesses to dispose of e-waste only through PCB-authorized recyclers like EWaste Kochi
  • DPDP Act 2023 mandates certified data destruction before disposal — non-compliance fines reach ₹250 crore per incident
  • Selling old IT equipment without data destruction exposes customer and employee data — creating immediate legal liability
  • Illegal e-waste dumping in Kerala carries prosecution under Environment Protection Act — criminal liability for directors
  • RBI, NABH and ISO auditors now require documented e-waste disposal records with PCB compliance proof
